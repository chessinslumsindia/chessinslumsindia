About Us

The Chess In Slums India is a Not for Profit Organization that dreams of enabling the development of cognitive abilities of young kids and the reduction of crimes and drug problems in young adults through chess education as a framework in the underprivileged areas of the country.

Founded in February 2023 by a small team of 3 motivated souls from different parts of the country, with a common theme connecting them, the love for chess. Discover the complete story of how we established this ambitious organization starting from scratch and get inspired yourself to contribute to the social mission we are on.
